Solid Nutrition London.

Learn. Train. Grow.

Summary.

Alina is a passionate personal trainer and nutritionist based in central London. She started Solid Nutrition London to provide a training and education service for local residents, assisting in the development of their physical and spiritual wellbeing, as well as to provide them with personally branded nutritional supplement products. What stood out to me about Alina was her passion for her clients and her firm belief in human connection. Keeping this in mind I designed Solid Nutrition London’s website to be a reflection of Alina herself and upon visiting the website, prospective clients can experience the closest thing to having already met Alina.

Brief.

  1. educate people on training and supplements through articles and regular content uploads.
  2. sell nutritional products.
  3. simple, intuitive design with minimal purchase process.
  4. target average gym users.

Solution.

Insight

Personal Branding

Reflecting on results from the Competitive Analysis, part of my recommendation was for Alina to brand herself as the face of Solid Nutrition London. Due to her business having been built from the ground up on word of mouth and Alina’s reputation for a person-centered business, my aim was to leverage visual design and personal branding to continue as close a personal experience as can be experienced via the web.

User Experience

Solid Nutrition London’s branding would not work without current & future clients experiencing something more than an online store - therefore part of the visual design also included an emphasis on a trainee’s 3 stage workout journey.

  • Learn.
  • Train.
  • Grow.
  • Clean interface for listing the article feed.
  • Small thumbnail for each article for visual appeal and context.
  • Overview of available services.
  • Example workouts specific to body type.
  • Minimalistic storefront.
  • Streamlined store to checkout flow.
  • Discount coupon feature.
  • Returning customer login.

WordPress as a Content Management System for ease of use, affordability and continued community development.

WooCommerce as a shop management tool for intuitive and affordable control of a small shopfront.
